Wine Around the World

Wine is more than just a drink — it’s a journey through culture, history, and tradition. From the sun-soaked vineyards of Italy and France to the bold expressions of South American terroirs, wine connects people across continents.

I’ve always been fascinated by wine, its regional flavors, and the experiences it creates. Although I am not a wine producer, I chose this theme for my project because I enjoy learning about the world of wine and its cultural significance.

By Priscila Parente
